Takeaway Framework

The CLEAR Framework: Is This AI Initiative Worth It?

Five dimensions every leader uses immediately ‚Äî in vendor meetings, board discussions, and internal investment decisions. Two or more red flags means redesign or deprioritize, regardless of how good the demo looks.

C

Complexity Match

Is AI actually the right tool, or would a simpler solution solve this?

‚ö† Vendor can't explain why AI beats a simpler approach
L

Leverage

How many decisions, how often, affecting how many dollars or people?

‚ö† Low-volume decisions ‚Äî AI won't pay back
E

Evidence of Performance

Tested on whose data? What are the accuracy and failure mode metrics?

‚ö† Demo data looks nothing like your operations
A

Access to Data

Do you have the data required? Is it clean, labeled, and legally yours?

‚ö† Requires data you don't have or can't share
R

Risk & Reversibility

What happens when the model is wrong? Who is accountable?

‚ö† High-stakes decisions with no human in the loop
